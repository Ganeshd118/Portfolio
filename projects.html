Business to Business (B2B) Users Workflow
Store Management

Sellers have the ability to create a store on the application and can select from a variety of customizable templates that best suit the look and feel of their store. Once the store is set up, sellers can easily share or refer their store details with potential customers or other sellers. Additionally, the platform offers a referral program where users are rewarded for every successful referral onboarding. These rewards can be accumulated and later redeemed, providing an incentive for users to actively promote and grow the community.

Choose your plan

Users are presented with a range of pricing models tailored to different needs. They can choose the most suitable option based on their store's capacity, ensuring they only pay for the features and resources that align with their business requirements. This flexibility allows for scalable growth and cost-efficiency as the store expands.

View More

To view the complete package details, click on the 'More' option. This will provide you with a detailed breakdown of all the features, inclusions, and benefits associated with the package, helping you make a more informed decision.

Dashboard

Seller Dashboard When a seller or business user clicks the link, they will be directed to the dashboard page. The seller can access the dashboard as a free user without needing to log in. From there, they can explore the application and review the available options without any restrictions.

Calendar and Scheduling

Seller Calendar and Scheduling Sellers can utilize the integrated calendar feature to schedule meetings and set reminders. This tool allows them to organize their time effectively by saving important meetings, deadlines, and tasks. Automatic reminders are sent for upcoming events, helping sellers stay on top of their schedule and manage their store more efficiently.
